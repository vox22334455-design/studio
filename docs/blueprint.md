# **App Name**: Ramadan Blessings

## Core Features:

- Daily Prayer Display: Displays a unique prayer (dua) for each day of Ramadan.
- Prayer Card Design: Showcase each prayer within an aesthetically pleasing card, enhanced with Islamic-inspired decorations, a gradient background, and a subtle golden star.
- Prayer Notification: Send out timely reminders 15 minutes before Maghrib (sunset) prayer, prompting users to reflect and engage with the day's prayer.
- Mecca Adhan Integration: Plays the authentic Mecca Adhan (call to prayer) at the designated prayer times based on the userâ€™s location. Option to mute from inside the app.
- Ramadan Ambience: Allows users to stream calming, spiritually uplifting audio to enhance their Ramadan experience. Selection of soft instrumental music or subtle ambient sounds.
- Eid Transition: Automates transition of the app from Ramadan mode into an Eid Al-Fitr themed experience.
- AI Content Adaptation Tool: Use an AI tool to analyze user engagement and preferences during Ramadan, adapting EL-Heliebi Verse promotional content (shown at end of Ramadan) for optimal appeal and relevance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#192375), evocative of the night sky and spiritual depth.
- Background color: Soft blue (#D1D9EB), very subtly shifted in hue and desaturated, creating a tranquil backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4A24E), used sparingly for highlights, stars, and text elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font providing a literary, slightly formal feel to complement the content.
- Elegant, minimalist icons will represent core functions, rendered in gold to contrast with the dark theme.
- Clean, card-based design with a focus on readability and ease of navigation. Daily prayers are prominently displayed.
- Subtle animations, such as gently twinkling stars and softly illuminating lanterns, to add depth and visual appeal without distraction.
